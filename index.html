How does it work?

A much more effective,and much more fun, method of learning vocabulary is through associating very visual images with something that sounds like the word you want to remember, also known as the mnemonics method.

Check out this example

-Let's take the Spanish word "debil", which translates to "weak".
-The Spanish "debil", sounds just like an English "devil".
-By creating the visual image of weak devil it will be much easier for you to rember this new word.

It is simply the most effective way to learn

Adding associations and creating patterns makes the material more meaningful ad memorable. By giving you associations and cues, mnemonics allow you to cross-reference the information in different parts of your memory, making it a very effective way to pick up new vocabulary.
